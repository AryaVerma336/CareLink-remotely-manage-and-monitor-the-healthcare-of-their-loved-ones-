How do I book an appointment from abroad?▼
Log in as Patient Relative, then go to Book Appointment. Choose city, hospital, specialty, doctor and time slot. Your parent receives an SMS reminder automatically.
How does GPS pickup work?▼
After booking, request Pickup from your dashboard. A verified driver is assigned. You track their GPS in real-time from anywhere in the world.
Is my parent's health data secure?▼
Yes. CareLink is HIPAA compliant with AES-256 encryption. Medical records are encrypted at rest and in transit. Only authorized family members can access the data.
How does the AI symptom checker work?▼
CareAI uses a clinically-validated symptom database to analyze reported symptoms, suggest urgency, and recommend appropriate specialists. It does not replace professional medical advice.
