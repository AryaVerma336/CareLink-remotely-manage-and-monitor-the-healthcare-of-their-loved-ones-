❤️ CareLink — Remote Healthcare Management System

Because care shouldn’t depend on distance.

CareLink is a digital healthcare management platform designed for individuals who live away from their loved ones but still want to actively manage their health and well-being. It bridges the gap between patients and caregivers through real-time monitoring, centralized records, and smart assistance tools.

⸻

🧠 Problem Statement

In today’s world, families are often separated due to:
	•	Jobs and education in different cities or countries
	•	Elderly parents living alone
	•	Limited access to consistent healthcare monitoring

This leads to:
	•	Poor health tracking
	•	Missed medications
	•	Delayed emergency response
	•	Lack of communication between caregivers and patients

👉 There is no simple, unified system to remotely manage a loved one’s healthcare effectively.

⸻

💡 Solution

CareLink provides a centralized platform that allows caregivers to:
	•	Monitor health status remotely
	•	Track medications and appointments
	•	Access medical records anytime
	•	Receive alerts and updates
	•	Communicate easily with patients
